## Built for the future, from the start.

Axos Financial empowers consumers and businesses to easily manage their financial lives. Through a blend of human insight and digital expertise, we help our customers plan, bank, borrow, and invest in a way that’s both convenient and customized.

More than two decades ago, Axos reinvented the banking model. By launching as a digital bank on July 4, 2000, we signaled our independence from traditional, brick-and-mortar banking. Since then, we've grown to over $28.2 billion in assets. By offering competitive interest rates, low to no fees, and convenient access, we've freed our customers from the constraints of traditional banking so they can focus on the important things in life.

Axos is FDIC-insured and headquartered in San Diego, California, with offices in Las Vegas, Nevada. Axos Bank affiliates include Axos Clearing LLC and Axos Invest, Inc., with offices in Los Angeles, California; Centennial, Colorado; Overland Park, Kansas; Omaha, Nebraska; Las Vegas, Nevada; New York, New York; Salt Lake City, Utah; and more coming soon. Our holding company, Axos Financial, Inc., is publicly traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol “AX” and is headquartered in Las Vegas, Nevada.
