where your money works for the life you want to live.

## The SoFi Story

- 2011![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2011-flag_icon%402x.png)

### Aug 2011



**Social Finance, Inc** is founded by Stanford business school students—originally using an alumni-funded lending model to connect recent grads with alumni in their community.


- 2012![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2012-grad_icon%402x.png)

### May 2012




SoFi introduces Student Loan Refinancing, becoming the first company to refinance federal and private student loans.


- 2013![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2012-grad_icon%402x.png)

### Apr 2013




SoFi expands student loan refinancing borrowing products, with increased flexibility and terms.


- 2014![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2014-home_icon%402x.png)

### Oct 2014




SoFi launches mortgages.


- 2015![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2015-wallet_icon%402x.png)

### 2015




SoFi begins offering personal loans and also becomes the first U.S-based fintech company to receive a $1 billion funding round.


- 2016![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2016-building_icon%402x.png)

### Sept 2016




SoFi announces their SoFi at Work program to offer employee benefits like student loan contributions and refinancing.


- 2017![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2017-clipboard_icon%402x.png)

### Oct 2017




SoFi announces a student loan refinancing product for medical residents and fellows.


- 2018![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/BR25-2376100_icon-2018-03.png)

### Mar 2018




Anthony Noto becomes SoFi Chief Executive Officer (CEO).


- 2019![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2019-phone_icon%402x.png)

### Jan 2019




SoFi launches SoFi Money® and SoFi Invest®.


- 2020![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/2020-cake_icon%402x.png)

### Jan 2020




SoFi celebrates 1,000,000 members.


- 2021![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/BR25-2376100_icon-2021-06.png)

### Jun 2021




SoFi becomes a publicly traded company on the Nasdaq Global Market ($SOFI on Nasdaq).


- 2022![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/BR25-2376100_icon-2022-06.png)

### Jun 2022




SoFi receives federal approval to become a national bank, providing members access to FDIC-insured checking and savings products, helping them protect and realize their financial ambitions.


- 2023![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/BR25-2376100_icon-2023-05.png)

### May 2023




SoFi launches SoFi Travel, powered by Expedia, to help members save money and earn rewards when booking travel plans.


- 2024![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/BR25-2376100_icon-2024-12.png)

### Dec 2024




SoFi celebrates the milestone of 10 million members getting their money right with us.


- 2025![](https://d32ijn7u0aqfv4.cloudfront.net/wp/wp-content/uploads/raw/BR25-2376100_icon-2025-02.png)

### Feb 2025




SoFi launches SoFi Plus, America’s most rewarding financial membership—all in one app.
